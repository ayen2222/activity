About

1. What is MACEC stand for?
MACEC means MARINDUQUE COUNCIL FOR ENVIRONMENTAL CONCERN


2. When MACEC is Established?
MACEC is a duly incorporated non-stock, non-profit and non-government multi-sectoral environmental organization established under the laws of the Republic of the Philippines and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission per Registration No. A1997-18465 on January 23, 1998. It envisions of “a clean, bountiful and life – giving environment in the Island – Province of Marinduque which benefits the present and future generations”.
